About me

I have 3 years of bachelor's degree and 2 years of master's degree at the Université lumière Lyon 2, specializing in translation and interpretation in English, French and Arabic.
I did an internship in French Arabic translation and interpretation at ALATA's company.
I have 5 years of experience in legal translation. I specialize in the translation of official documents such as birth, marriage, divorce, driver's license, legal funds, etc.

Origins

Syrian by origin, I arrived in France in 2013 with my parents. When we arrived, we would have liked to have had someone to translate what we wanted to say and help us with our procedures.
This personal experience encouraged me to continue my studies in the field of translation.
On one hand, that helped me improving my language skills and to discover the field of translation in which I'm working.
And on the other hand, it allowed me to help foreigners communicate better in French and make themselves better understood.
